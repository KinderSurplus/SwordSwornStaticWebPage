SWORD SWORN: THE GAME

About:

Sword Sworn is about a girl who comes across a sentient magical sword. Her mission is to transport this sword to a portal across the country and throw it into a portal to another dimension. But of course, it's never that easy, is it? As she travels, she must use this sword to defend herself from the demons trying to stop them from completing their journey. Will they make it? It's up to you now!

Main Character:

Amira Zain. She may look brave, but she's very afraid. You should be too...

Weapon:

A mysterious magical sword.
